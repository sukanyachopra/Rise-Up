Future Features

  • If any student gives a mock test on our test and assessment platform, then the pattern of the mistakes made by the student will be identified based on the keywords extracted from the questions and more similar questions will be recommended for practice in the form of an assignment.
  • In the upcoming tests, the student will face 20% of the questions from the improvement assignment given to the student.
  • After every 3-4 tests, there will be a test of all the wrong questions done by the student.
  • Question recommendation system based on the frequency of the question/topics being asked in previous exams.
  • For doubt resolution system, those doubts will be prioritized where the students have also posted their approach. This will ensure that students make their own efforts in solving the questions.
